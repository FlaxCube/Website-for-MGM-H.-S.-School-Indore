Streams after 10th

Discover specialized streams tailored to students' interests: PCM for science and engineering, PCB for life sciences and medicine, and Commerce for business and finance.
PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths)

Ideal for engineering and technology enthusiasts.

Physics helps understand how things work in the world, Chemistry deals with substances and their properties, and Mathematics is used for problem-solving and analysis. Together, these subjects help solve real-world problems, design new technologies, and make scientific discoveries.

PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Bio)

Perfect for aspiring medical and healthcare professionals.

Physics helps understand the laws of nature, Chemistry deals with the composition and reactions of substances, and Biology explores living organisms and their functions. These subjects are crucial for diagnosing illnesses, developing treatments, and understanding the natural world.

Commerce

For those interested in finance, entrepreneurship, and business management.

Economics helps comprehend how money flows in society, Accountancy involves keeping track of financial records, and Business Studies covers topics like marketing and management. Together, these subjects are fundamental for careers in finance, entrepreneurship, and business management.
